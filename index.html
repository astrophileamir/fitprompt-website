Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 22, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Fitprompt! This Privacy Policy explains how Fitprompt ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our iOS mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and care.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you use the Fitprompt App. This includes:

  • User-Provided Data: During the onboarding process, we collect personal information such as your age, weight, height, gender, specific fitness goals (e.g., muscle gain, weight loss), dietary preferences (e.g., vegetarian, vegan, allergies), and current activity level. This data is essential for generating your personalized fitness and nutrition plans.
  • Chat History: When you interact with our 24/7 AI chat coach, your chat history is stored to provide conversational context and improve the relevance of the AI's responses regarding your personalized plan.
  • Non-Personal Analytics Data: We may also collect non-personal, aggregated data related to app usage, such as crash reports, feature usage metrics, and general interaction patterns. This data helps us understand how the App is used, identify areas for improvement, and enhance the overall user experience. This data does not identify you personally.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used primarily to provide and improve the Fitprompt service:

  • To generate and deliver highly personalized multi-week workout and diet plans tailored to your unique profile.
  • To enable the 24/7 AI chat coach to provide contextual and relevant answers based on your fitness plan and previous interactions.
  • To analyze app performance, identify bugs, and improve the functionality and features of Fitprompt.
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our services.

Data Storage and Security

Your user data, including onboarding information and chat history, is stored securely using Google's Firestore, a robust and scalable cloud NoSQL database service. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Sharing with Third Parties

To power the AI features within Fitprompt, specifically the personalized plan generation and the AI chat coach, we utilize the OpenAI API. Please be aware of the following:

  • OpenAI API: Anonymized user data, specifically your onboarding answers (e.g., fitness goals, dietary preferences, activity level, gender, age range, weight range, height range – but NOT exact values or personally identifying information) and chat messages (stripped of any explicit personal identifiers you might accidentally include), are securely sent to the OpenAI API.
  • Secure Proxy: This data transmission occurs through a secure Supabase Edge Function proxy. This proxy adds an additional layer of security and ensures that personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or device identifiers are NOT sent to OpenAI.
  • OpenAI's use of data is governed by their own privacy policy. While we take measures to anonymize and proxy data, we encourage you to review OpenAI's Privacy Policy for more information.

We do not sell, rent, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share aggregated or de-identified information that cannot reasonably be used to identify you.

In-App Purchases

Fitprompt offers a one-time In-App Purchase to unlock full functionality. All payment transactions are securely processed by Apple through the App Store. Fitprompt does not collect or store your payment information, credit card details, or financial data. This information is handled exclusively by Apple in accordance with their terms and privacy policies.

Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You have the right to request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data. Please note that fulfilling a deletion request may lead to the loss of your personalized plans and chat history, as this data is integral to the App's functionality.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at fitprompt@outlook.com. We will respond to your request in accordance with applicable laws.
